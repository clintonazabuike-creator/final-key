# final-key🗝️ The Final Key: Digital Legacy Vault
Engineered for Human Security By ClintonAzabuike-Creator

The Final Key is a server-less emergency utility designed to solve the problem of digital inheritance. It allows individuals to prepare sensitive instructions and access codes for their loved ones in a way that is 100% private.

🛡️ Security Protocol
Zero-Cloud Storage: We do not store your secrets. The app processes your text locally in the browser memory.

Local Generation: The "Protocol File" is generated as a Blob on your device. It never touches a database.

Sovereign Control: You control the file. You decide where it is stored (USB, Printed, or Encrypted Drive).

💡 Why This Matters
In a world of two-factor authentication and encrypted devices, families are often locked out of essential information during a crisis. The Final Key provides a high-tech, yet simple way to ensure your legacy and responsibilities are managed when you are unable to do so yourself.
